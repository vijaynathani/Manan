1. Use the right-hand thumb to move the lower beads up to the bar and the right-hand index finger to move the lower beads away from the bar. The upper bead is moved bidirectionally by the index finger.
  2. For direct addition, move towards the bar. For direct subtraction, move away from the bar.
  3. When direct addition/subtraction is unfeasible, use 5's complement to move beads down/up, respectively. (1,4) and (2,3) are 5's complement groups.
    • +1 = +5 -4 and -1 = -5 +4
    • +2 = +5 -3 and -2 = -5 +3
    • +3 = +5 -2 and -3 = -5 +2
    • +4 = +5 -1 and -4 = -5 +1
  4. When 5's complement is unfeasible, use 10's complement. (1,9), (2,8), (3,7), (4,6), and (5,5) are 10's complement groups.
    • +1= +10 -9 and -1 = -10 +9
    • +2= +10 -8 and -2 = -10 +8
    • +3= +10 -7 and -3 = -10 +7
    • +4= +10 -6 and -4 = -10 +6
    • +5= +10 -5 and -5 = -10 +5
    • +6= +10 -4 and -6 = -10 +4
    • +7= +10 -3 and -7 = -10 +3
    • +8= +10 -2 and -8 = -10 +2
    • +9= +10 -1 and -9 = -10 +1
